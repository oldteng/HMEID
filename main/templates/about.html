    Introduction of HMEID

    HMEID is a database for providing comprehensive resources of human mobile element insertions (MEIs). MEIs collected in HMEID were detected by processing Whole Genome Sequencing (WGS) data from 1KGP and Nyuwa datasets.


    How to Cite

    Niu, Y., Teng, X., Shi, Y., Li, Y., Tang, Y., Zhang, P., ... & Han100K Initiative. (2021). Genome-wide analysis of mobile element insertions in human genomes. bioRxiv.
